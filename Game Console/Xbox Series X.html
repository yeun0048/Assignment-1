Xbox Series X

The Xbox Series X are the fourth generation of consoles in the Xbox series, succeeding the previous generation's Xbox One. Released on November 10, 2020, the higher-end Xbox Series X are part of the ninth generation of video game consoles, which also includes Sony's PlayStation 5, released the same month.
